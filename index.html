⚡ Combat Mechanics

The combat system features real-time action with critical hits, dodge chance, and damage variation. When you attack, a hitbox is created in front of your character. Enemies within range will take damage. Critical hits deal bonus damage and cannot be dodged.

  • • Attack: Click mouse or press Space
  • • Critical Hit: Random chance based on your critical stat
  • • Damage: Based on attack stat minus enemy defense
  • • Hit Chance: Based on your hit stat vs enemy flee stat
